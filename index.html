Becoming the GREATEST to ever do it!

Drawing has been my passion for as long as I can remember. Recently, near the end of last year, I was inspired by the Animator/Youtuber Telepurte. They did a project where they created an nimation everyday! So, I decided to start making/drawing sketches everyday myself :^)

It was easy when I first started beacause I had so many ideas and wanted to practice certain styles and techniques when drawing. However, as the days past by, I started running out of things to draw and brainstorming was what I had to do to get something worth drawing. I could easily draw a star, moon, house, cat, dog, table, etc. But drawing something so simple would only take 10-15 mins, but even worse is that I wouldn't be practicing anything worthwhile if I drew someting so simple. If I wanted to practice drawing hands, then a hand holding a pencil would be a great idea. . . . but it would still be extremely simple and I wouldn't gain anything from it. Then, I called upon terA and Guessing Panda (My brother and sister), to help me come up with amazing ideas and I get to share my pictures and ideas with them to improve and reshape my drawings.
